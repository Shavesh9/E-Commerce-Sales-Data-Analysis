# E-Commerce Sales Data Analysis Report

## Project Overview

The objective of this project was to analyze e-commerce sales data and derive actionable business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, product demand, geographic trends, and payment preferences.

The analysis was performed using Python libraries including Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn.

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains the following fields:

* Order ID
* Customer ID
* Gender
* Age
* Product Category
* Product Name
* Quantity
* Price
* Order Date
* Payment Method
* City
* Rating

A new column named **Sales** was created using:

Sales = Quantity × Price

---

## 1. Sales Trend Analysis

### Objective

Analyze sales performance over time and identify trends.

### Methodology

* Converted the Order Date column to datetime format.
* Aggregated sales on a monthly basis.
* Visualized sales trends using a line chart.

### Findings

* Sales showed fluctuations across different months.
* Certain months recorded significantly higher revenue, indicating possible seasonal demand.
* The trend analysis helps identify peak business periods and supports future sales forecasting.

---

## 2. Top-Selling Products and Categories

### Objective

Identify products and categories generating the highest revenue.

### Methodology

* Grouped sales by Product Name.
* Grouped sales by Product Category.
* Ranked products and categories based on total sales.

### Findings

* A small number of products contributed a large portion of total revenue.
* Electronics and Home categories were among the strongest-performing categories.
* Identifying top products can help optimize inventory and marketing strategies.

---

## 3. Customer Segmentation Analysis

### Objective

Understand customer purchasing behavior based on demographics.

### Methodology

* Segmented customers into age groups.
* Analyzed sales by gender.
* Compared revenue contributions across customer segments.

### Findings

* Middle-aged customers contributed the highest sales revenue.
* Sales distribution between male and female customers was relatively balanced.
* Customer segmentation provides valuable insights for targeted marketing campaigns.

---

## 4. City-Wise Sales Performance

### Objective

Evaluate geographical sales performance.

### Methodology

* Grouped sales by city.
* Ranked cities based on total revenue generated.

### Findings

* Some cities generated significantly higher revenue than others.
* Top-performing cities can be prioritized for expansion and promotional activities.
* Lower-performing cities may require additional marketing efforts.

---

## 5. Payment Method Preferences

### Objective

Analyze customer payment behavior.

### Methodology

* Grouped sales by payment method.
* Calculated revenue contribution of each payment option.
* Visualized payment method distribution.

### Findings

* Credit Card transactions generated the highest revenue.
* Digital payment methods were widely adopted.
* Understanding payment preferences helps improve customer experience and payment infrastructure.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Focus marketing efforts on top-performing product categories.
2. Maintain adequate inventory levels for best-selling products.
3. Develop targeted campaigns for high-value customer age groups.
4. Increase promotional activities in underperforming cities.
5. Encourage digital payment adoption through discounts and cashback offers.
6. Use sales trend insights for demand forecasting and inventory planning.
